Introduction

Lionel Messi, often regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time, has achieved numerous feats in his illustrious career. Born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, Messi began his career at FC Barcelona, where he became their all-time top scorer.

Career Highlights

  • 2004: Debuted for FC Barcelona at age 17.
  • 2009: Won his first UEFA Champions League with Barcelona.
  • 2012: Set a world record for most goals in a calendar year with 91 goals.
  • 2021: Signed with Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) after leaving Barcelona.

Achievements

Messi's awards include:

  • 8 Ballon d'Or awards (as of 2024)
  • 4 UEFA Champions League titles
  • 3 FIFA Club World Cups
  • 2 Copa America with Argentina (2021)(2024)
  • Numerous La Liga titles with Barcelona

Famous Quotes
